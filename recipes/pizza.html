pizza recipe

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish featuring a round, thin, or thick crust topped with a savory tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings. Classic pizzas often include fresh mozzarella, basil, and olive oil for a Margherita style, or pepperoni for a spicier, meatier flavor. The crust, crispy on the outside and soft within, holds each layer of toppings together. Baked to perfection, pizza is known for its mouthwatering blend of gooey cheese, rich sauce, and satisfying crunch, making it a universal favorite for any meal or occasion.

ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (or marinara sauce)

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil

  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for Margherita style)

  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Optional toppings

  • Pepperoni slices
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Black olives
  • Red onion slices
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (for a bit of heat)

cooking steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and, if using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.

  2. Prepare the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness, then transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza peel (if using a pizza stone).

  3. Add Sauce and Cheese: Spread an even layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly on top.

  4. Add Toppings: Add any additional toppings you like, such as pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, or olives.

  5. Bake: Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza, then bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven, add fresh basil if desired, and let it cool slightly. Slice and enjoy!
